# Wind Turbine Inspection Procedures

## Overview

Wind turbine inspections are critical for ensuring safe, reliable, and efficient operation throughout the turbine's 20-25 year design life. A structured inspection program identifies wear, damage, and potential failures before they lead to costly downtime or safety hazards. Inspections range from daily visual checks performed by site technicians to comprehensive annual inspections requiring specialized equipment and certified personnel.

## Types of Inspections

### Visual Inspection (Walk-Around)

A visual inspection is performed from ground level or via remote monitoring. Technicians observe the turbine exterior for obvious signs of damage, leaks, or abnormal behavior.

**Frequency:** Daily or after severe weather events.

**Checklist:**
- Check tower base for cracks, corrosion, or water ingress
- Observe blade surfaces for visible damage, erosion, or ice buildup
- Listen for unusual noises from the nacelle or drivetrain
- Verify aviation warning lights are operational
- Inspect the foundation for settlement, cracking, or vegetation overgrowth
- Confirm the wind measurement instruments (anemometer, wind vane) are intact
- Review SCADA alerts and alarms from the previous 24 hours

### Regular Scheduled Checks (Monthly/Quarterly)

These inspections go beyond visual observation and involve accessing the turbine interior.

**Checklist:**
- Inspect tower bolt torque on a sample basis
- Check hydraulic and lubrication fluid levels
- Inspect electrical cable routing for chafing or wear
- Review oil filter condition and differential pressure
- Verify fire suppression system status indicators
- Test emergency stop buttons at tower base and nacelle
- Inspect climb-assist and safety systems (ladders, fall arrest)

### Annual Comprehensive Inspection

The annual inspection is the most thorough and typically involves a multi-day effort per turbine. It covers every major subsystem.

**Scope includes:**
- Full blade inspection (rope access or drone-based)
- Drivetrain endoscopy (gearbox internals)
- Electrical system thermography
- Bolt torque verification on all critical joints
- Foundation structural assessment
- Yaw and pitch system functional testing
- Safety system recertification

## Component-Specific Inspections

### Blade Inspection

Blades are inspected for leading-edge erosion, lightning strike damage, cracks, delamination, and surface coating degradation.

**Methods:**
- **Ground-based photography** using telephoto lenses
- **Drone inspection** with high-resolution cameras
- **Rope access** by certified technicians for hands-on assessment
- **Ultrasonic testing** for internal delamination detection

**Key areas of focus:**
- Leading edge (erosion from rain, sand, insects)
- Trailing edge (bonding line integrity)
- Root section (bolt connections and laminate condition)
- Tip area (lightning receptor condition)
- Drainage holes (blockage check)

### Tower Inspection

**External checks:**
- Surface coating and paint condition
- Weld seam integrity (visual and dye-penetrant testing)
- Corrosion at flanges and door frames
- Grout condition at the base flange

**Internal checks:**
- Bolt torque at flange connections (top, middle, base)
- Internal ladder and platform structural integrity
- Cable tray and cable condition
- Internal condensation or water accumulation
- Lighting and ventilation systems

### Foundation Inspection

- Visible cracks in the concrete pedestal
- Settlement or tilting measurement
- Grout condition between tower base and foundation
- Anchor bolt tension verification
- Drainage system functionality
- Ground erosion around the foundation perimeter

### Nacelle and Drivetrain Inspection

- Gearbox oil analysis (particle count, water content, viscosity)
- Generator bearing temperature and vibration trending
- Main bearing grease condition
- Brake pad and disc wear measurement
- Coupling alignment check
- Nacelle cover and seal integrity
- Cooling system performance (radiators, fans, heat exchangers)

### Electrical Systems Inspection

- Transformer oil level and condition
- Switchgear contact wear and arc damage
- Cable termination torque and thermographic scan
- Grounding system continuity test
- Surge protection device status
- Converter cooling system inspection
- Battery backup and UPS functionality test

## Documentation and Reporting

All inspection findings must be documented with photographs, measurements, and severity classifications. The standard severity levels are:

- **Category 1 (Critical):** Immediate action required; turbine shutdown if necessary
- **Category 2 (Major):** Repair required within 30 days
- **Category 3 (Minor):** Schedule repair at next planned maintenance
- **Category 4 (Observation):** Monitor at next inspection

# Quy Trinh Kiem Tra Tuabin Gio

## Tong Quan

Kiem tra tuabin gio la yeu to then chot de dam bao van hanh an toan, dang tin cay va hieu qua trong suot vong doi thiet ke 20-25 nam cua tuabin. Chuong trinh kiem tra co cau truc giup phat hien hao mon, hu hong va cac su co tiem an truoc khi chung dan den thoi gian ngung hoat dong ton kem hoac nguy hiem ve an toan. Cac cuoc kiem tra bao gom tu kiem tra truc quan hang ngay den kiem tra toan dien hang nam yeu cau thiet bi chuyen dung va nhan su co chung chi.

## Cac Loai Kiem Tra

### Kiem Tra Truc Quan (Di Quanh)

Kiem tra truc quan duoc thuc hien tu mat dat hoac qua giam sat tu xa. Ky thuat vien quan sat ben ngoai tuabin de phat hien cac dau hieu hu hong, ro ri hoac hoat dong bat thuong.

**Tan suat:** Hang ngay hoac sau cac su kien thoi tiet khac nghiet.

**Danh sach kiem tra:**
- Kiem tra chan thap xem co nut, an mon hoac tham nuoc khong
- Quan sat be mat canh xem co hu hong, xoi mon hoac dong bang khong
- Lang nghe tieng on bat thuong tu vo tuabin hoac he truyen dong
- Xac nhan den canh bao hang khong hoat dong
- Kiem tra mong xem co lun, nut hoac cay co moc qua khong
- Xac nhan cac thiet bi do gio (may do gio, chong gio) con nguyen ven
- Xem xet canh bao va bao dong SCADA tu 24 gio truoc

### Kiem Tra Dinh Ky (Hang Thang/Hang Quy)

Cac cuoc kiem tra nay vuot ra ngoai quan sat truc quan va bao gom viec truy cap ben trong tuabin.

**Danh sach kiem tra:**
- Kiem tra mo-men xoan bu-long thap theo mau
- Kiem tra muc dau thuy luc va dau boi tron
- Kiem tra tuyen cap dien xem co bi co sat hoac moi mon khong
- Xem xet tinh trang bo loc dau va ap suat vi sai
- Xac nhan chi bao trang thai he thong chua chay
- Thu nut dung khan cap tai chan thap va vo tuabin
- Kiem tra he thong ho tro leo va he thong an toan

### Kiem Tra Toan Dien Hang Nam

Kiem tra hang nam la cuoc kiem tra ky luong nhat va thuong mat nhieu ngay cho moi tuabin.

**Pham vi bao gom:**
- Kiem tra canh day du (tiep can bang day hoac bang drone)
- Noi soi he truyen dong (ben trong hop so)
- Chup nhiet he thong dien
- Xac nhan mo-men xoan bu-long tren tat ca cac moi noi quan trong
- Danh gia ket cau mong
- Thu chuc nang he thong quay va goc canh
- Tai chung nhan he thong an toan

## Kiem Tra Theo Thanh Phan Cu The

### Kiem Tra Canh

Canh duoc kiem tra ve xoi mon mep truoc, hu hong do set danh, nut, tach lop va suy giam lop phu be mat.

**Phuong phap:**
- Chup anh tu mat dat bang ong kinh tele
- Kiem tra bang drone voi camera do phan giai cao
- Tiep can bang day boi ky thuat vien co chung chi
- Kiem tra sieu am de phat hien tach lop ben trong

### Kiem Tra Thap

- Tinh trang lop son va phu be mat
- Toan ven moi han
- An mon tai cac mat bich va khung cua
- Tinh trang vua tai mat bich day

### Kiem Tra Mong

- Vet nut nhin thay tren be mong
- Do lun hoac nghieng
- Tinh trang vua giua chan thap va mong
- Xac nhan luc cang bu-long neo
- Chuc nang he thong thoat nuoc

### Kiem Tra He Thong Dien

- Muc va tinh trang dau bien ap
- Moi mon tiep diem tu dong cat va hu hong do ho quang
- Kiem tra nhiet do cac dau noi cap
- Thu tinh lien tuc he thong noi dat
- Trang thai thiet bi chong set

## Tai Lieu va Bao Cao

Tat ca cac phat hien kiem tra phai duoc ghi lai bang hinh anh, so do va phan loai muc do nghiem trong:

- **Muc 1 (Nghiem Trong):** Yeu cau hanh dong ngay lap tuc; dung tuabin neu can
- **Muc 2 (Lon):** Yeu cau sua chua trong vong 30 ngay
- **Muc 3 (Nho):** Len lich sua chua tai lan bao tri ke hoach tiep theo
- **Muc 4 (Quan Sat):** Theo doi tai lan kiem tra tiep theo
