# Wind Turbine Maintenance Schedule

## Overview

A well-structured maintenance schedule is essential for maximizing wind turbine availability, minimizing unplanned downtime, and extending component life. Maintenance activities are organized by time interval, ranging from daily operational checks to major annual overhauls. Each interval targets specific systems and components based on manufacturer recommendations, regulatory requirements, and operational experience.

## Daily Maintenance Tasks

Daily tasks are typically performed by on-site operations personnel and focus on monitoring and basic upkeep.

- Review SCADA system for active alarms, warnings, and performance deviations
- Monitor wind turbine availability and power output against expected values
- Check meteorological data quality (anemometer, wind vane, temperature sensors)
- Verify remote communication links are operational
- Review any automatic restart events or fault logs
- Perform a ground-level visual walk-around if personnel are on-site
- Confirm aviation obstruction lights are functioning

## Weekly Maintenance Tasks

Weekly tasks involve slightly more detailed checks and data review.

- Analyze turbine performance data for trending anomalies
- Review vibration monitoring data from CMS (Condition Monitoring System)
- Check oil and hydraulic fluid levels remotely via SCADA where available
- Verify battery backup system voltage and charge status
- Inspect site access roads and security fencing
- Review and acknowledge any deferred SCADA alarms
- Check fire detection and suppression system status indicators

## Monthly Maintenance Tasks

Monthly maintenance requires physical access to the turbine and hands-on checks.

- Inspect hydraulic power unit: fluid level, filter condition, hose integrity
- Check lubrication system: grease levels, pump operation, distribution lines
- Inspect tower internal environment: temperature, humidity, condensation
- Test emergency stop buttons at all locations (tower base, mid-tower, nacelle)
- Verify UPS and backup power systems with a load test
- Inspect cable trays and cable routing for chafing or displacement
- Check door seals, hatches, and weather protection on the nacelle
- Review oil and grease consumption rates against expected values
- Clean air intake filters on nacelle cooling systems

## Quarterly Maintenance Tasks (Every 3 Months)

Quarterly tasks involve more detailed mechanical and electrical checks.

- Perform gearbox oil sampling and send for laboratory analysis (particle count, water content, viscosity, acid number)
- Inspect pitch system: hydraulic cylinders or electric pitch motors, pitch bearings, backup power
- Check yaw system: brake pad wear, yaw motor condition, yaw bearing grease
- Inspect main shaft bearing: temperature trending, grease condition
- Verify torque on a sample of critical bolted connections
- Test the manual and automatic fire suppression systems
- Inspect the transformer: oil level, silica gel breather, cooling fans
- Check converter cabinet: dust accumulation, fan operation, capacitor condition
- Verify grid protection relay settings and test trip functions
- Inspect lightning protection system: down conductors, grounding connections

## Semi-Annual Maintenance Tasks (Every 6 Months)

Semi-annual maintenance covers deeper inspections and preventive replacements.

- Perform comprehensive brake system inspection: pad thickness, disc condition, caliper operation
- Inspect generator: bearing condition, winding insulation resistance (megger test), brush wear (for DFIG)
- Replace hydraulic filters and check system pressure settings
- Inspect all safety systems: fall arrest equipment, rescue devices, first aid supplies
- Perform yaw bearing grease replenishment
- Inspect and clean nacelle anemometer and wind vane
- Check main power cable connections with thermographic imaging
- Test the complete pitch system emergency feathering function
- Inspect blade root bolts with calibrated torque wrench (sample check)
- Verify crane and hoist equipment certification

## Annual Maintenance Tasks

The annual service is the most extensive and typically requires 2-5 days per turbine with a team of 2-4 technicians.

### Mechanical Systems
- Complete gearbox inspection: endoscopy of gear teeth and bearings, oil flush and replacement
- Main bearing inspection and grease replacement
- Full brake system overhaul: replace pads if below minimum thickness
- Yaw bearing inspection and re-greasing
- Pitch bearing inspection and re-greasing
- Coupling alignment verification (generator-gearbox coupling)
- Check all vibration damper condition (tower, nacelle)

### Electrical Systems
- Complete switchgear inspection and contact maintenance
- Transformer oil sampling and dissolved gas analysis (DGA)
- Full thermographic survey of all electrical connections
- Insulation resistance testing on generator windings
- Surge protection device replacement if triggered
- Complete grounding system resistance measurement
- Power quality measurement and harmonic analysis

### Structural Systems
- Full blade inspection (drone or rope access): leading edge, trailing edge, root, tip
- Tower bolt torque verification at all flange levels
- Foundation inspection: crack mapping, settlement measurement
- Nacelle frame and bedplate inspection for cracks or deformation
- Tower coating condition assessment

### Control and Safety Systems
- SCADA system calibration and sensor verification
- CMS sensor calibration and data quality check
- Complete safety system recertification
- Update control software if manufacturer releases new versions
- Backup all turbine controller parameters and settings

## Major Overhaul (Every 5 Years)

At five-year intervals, additional major maintenance activities are performed:

- Gearbox major service or replacement assessment
- Generator major service including bearing replacement
- Full blade leading-edge protection repair or replacement
- Tower internal coating renewal where needed
- Comprehensive bolt retorque of all structural connections
- Yaw and pitch bearing replacement assessment

# Lich Bao Tri Tuabin Gio

## Tong Quan

Lich bao tri co cau truc tot la yeu to thiet yeu de toi da hoa kha dung cua tuabin gio, giam thieu thoi gian ngung hoat dong ngoai ke hoach va keo dai tuoi tho linh kien. Cac hoat dong bao tri duoc to chuc theo khoang thoi gian, tu kiem tra van hanh hang ngay den dai tu hang nam.

## Nhiem Vu Bao Tri Hang Ngay

- Xem xet he thong SCADA de phat hien canh bao va sai lech hieu suat
- Giam sat tinh kha dung va san luong dien cua tuabin
- Kiem tra chat luong du lieu khi tuong
- Xac nhan lien lac tu xa hoat dong
- Xem xet cac su kien khoi dong lai tu dong hoac nhat ky loi
- Thuc hien kiem tra truc quan tu mat dat
- Xac nhan den canh bao hang khong hoat dong

## Nhiem Vu Bao Tri Hang Tuan

- Phan tich du lieu hieu suat tuabin de phat hien xu huong bat thuong
- Xem xet du lieu giam sat rung dong tu CMS
- Kiem tra muc dau va dau thuy luc tu xa qua SCADA
- Xac nhan dien ap va trang thai sac pin du phong
- Kiem tra duong vao cong truong va hang rao an ninh

## Nhiem Vu Bao Tri Hang Thang

- Kiem tra bo nguon thuy luc: muc dau, tinh trang bo loc, toan ven ong
- Kiem tra he thong boi tron: muc mo, hoat dong bom, duong phan phoi
- Kiem tra moi truong ben trong thap: nhiet do, do am, ngung tu
- Thu nut dung khan cap tai tat ca cac vi tri
- Xac nhan he thong UPS va nguon du phong bang thu tai
- Kiem tra khay cap va tuyen cap xem co bi co sat khong
- Kiem tra ron cua, nap va bao ve thoi tiet tren vo tuabin
- Lam sach bo loc khong khi tren he thong lam mat vo tuabin

## Nhiem Vu Bao Tri Hang Quy (3 Thang Mot Lan)

- Lay mau dau hop so va gui phan tich phong thi nghiem
- Kiem tra he thong goc canh: xi-lanh thuy luc hoac dong co dien, vong bi, nguon du phong
- Kiem tra he thong quay huong: do moi mon ma phanh, tinh trang dong co, mo vong bi
- Kiem tra vong bi truc chinh: xu huong nhiet do, tinh trang mo
- Xac nhan mo-men xoan tren mau cac moi noi bu-long quan trong
- Thu he thong chua chay thu cong va tu dong
- Kiem tra bien ap: muc dau, bo hut am silica gel, quat lam mat
- Kiem tra tu bien tan: bui tich tu, hoat dong quat, tinh trang tu dien

## Nhiem Vu Bao Tri Nua Nam (6 Thang Mot Lan)

- Kiem tra toan dien he thong phanh
- Kiem tra may phat: tinh trang vong bi, dien tro cach dien cuon day, do moi mon choi than
- Thay bo loc thuy luc va kiem tra cai dat ap suat
- Kiem tra tat ca he thong an toan: thiet bi chong roi, thiet bi cuu ho
- Bo sung mo vong bi quay huong
- Kiem tra va lam sach may do gio va chong gio tren vo tuabin
- Kiem tra ket noi cap nguon chinh bang chup nhiet
- Thu chuc nang canh quay ve vi tri an toan khan cap

## Nhiem Vu Bao Tri Hang Nam

Dich vu hang nam la toan dien nhat va thuong yeu cau 2-5 ngay cho moi tuabin voi doi ngu 2-4 ky thuat vien.

### He Thong Co Khi
- Kiem tra hop so hoan chinh: noi soi rang va vong bi, xa va thay dau
- Kiem tra vong bi chinh va thay mo
- Dai tu he thong phanh hoan chinh
- Kiem tra vong bi quay huong va tra mo
- Kiem tra vong bi goc canh va tra mo
- Xac nhan can chinh khop noi

### He Thong Dien
- Kiem tra tu dong cat hoan chinh va bao tri tiep diem
- Lay mau dau bien ap va phan tich khi hoa tan (DGA)
- Khao sat nhiet do hoan chinh tat ca cac ket noi dien
- Do dien tro cach dien cuon day may phat
- Thay thiet bi chong set neu da kich hoat
- Do dien tro he thong noi dat hoan chinh

### He Thong Ket Cau
- Kiem tra canh day du bang drone hoac tiep can bang day
- Xac nhan mo-men xoan bu-long thap tai tat ca cac muc mat bich
- Kiem tra mong: lap ban do vet nut, do lun
- Kiem tra khung vo tuabin va tam nen xem co nut hoac bien dang khong

### He Thong Dieu Khien va An Toan
- Hieu chuan he thong SCADA va xac nhan cam bien
- Hieu chuan cam bien CMS va kiem tra chat luong du lieu
- Tai chung nhan he thong an toan hoan chinh
- Cap nhat phan mem dieu khien neu nha san xuat phat hanh phien ban moi

## Dai Tu Lon (5 Nam Mot Lan)

- Dich vu lon hop so hoac danh gia thay the
- Dich vu lon may phat bao gom thay vong bi
- Sua chua hoac thay the bao ve mep truoc canh
- Lam moi lop phu ben trong thap khi can
- Siet lai bu-long toan dien tat ca cac ket noi ket cau
